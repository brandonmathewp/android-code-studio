## Android Device Requirements

The following requirements outline the minimum and recommended specifications for running the Android Code Studio smoothly on Android devices.

---

### Minimum Requirements
These specifications represent the lowest supported configuration. Performance may be limited, especially for large projects or advanced features.

1. **Operating System:**  
   Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher (SDK 26+)
2. **Memory (RAM):**  
   4–6 GB RAM
3. **Storage:**  
   8–10 GB of available free storage
4. **Processor (CPU):**  
   Mid-range CPU (64-bit architecture recommended)

---

### Recommended Requirements
These specifications are recommended for an optimal development experience, improved performance, and better stability.

1. **Operating System:**  
   Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher (SDK 26+)
2. **Memory (RAM):**  
   8–12 GB RAM
3. **Storage:**  
   At least 15 GB of available free storage
4. **Processor (CPU):**  
   Mid-range or higher CPU (64-bit architecture recommended)

---

### Notes
- Devices meeting only the minimum requirements may experience slower build times and reduced responsiveness.
- Additional storage may be required depending on project size, dependencies, and cached build artifacts.
